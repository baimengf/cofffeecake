EveryPicture - interactive website

Interactive Website
Mock Prototype

Step into a digital journey down memory lane with EveryPicture: the project is designed to evoke nostalgia by seamlessly blending modern design with the timeless charm of polaroid aesthetics. As users navigate through our website, they'll notice a unique overlay featuring a polaroid background, adding a touch of retro flair to their experience. I'm constantly refining the website to ensure optimal performance, including addressing issues like slow image duplication on slower internet connections. In addition to enhancing functionality, I'm also focusing on improving user engagement. The animations enhance the overlay experience and descriptive text/icons guide the user through the site, eliminating any sense of confusion. I also tried to gamify the experience with the feedback feature. Users can share reflections and random thoughts inspired by the images by simply clicking on the "feedback" button. Exploring images from different locations categorized for easy navigation offers a diverse and immersive journey through the collection of images.

Conclusion

Adjust button wording to clarify their purpose, e.g., "send me a message." Enhance button visibility by improving contrast with the background. Integrate feedback section into slider overlay for improved clarity and accessibility. Consider adding functionality to the doodle to enhance user engagement. Provide descriptive text for gallery images to enhance user understanding.
Overall, the prototype design is effective, but improvements are needed to enhance button visibility, clarify button functions, and integrate feedback section for improved usability. Incorporating user suggestions will likely result in a more intuitive and user-friendly design.
