Design Brief

Introducing a solution to address resource scarcity among homeless and immigrant communities. Collaborating with shelters, the app facilitates item requests, ensuring accessibility for all. Additionally, medical and hygiene supplies are available, catering to diverse community needs. The design focuses on simplicity and inclusivity, with streamlined navigation and options for both donation and request modes. Users can choose their preferred mode upon registration, tailoring their experience accordingly. The app aims to make a tangible impact by connecting donors with those in need, fostering a sense of community support and empowerment.
